Backrooms 1: level 0 "Tutorial"

By Quentin Kirschner and Jackson Hicks

Introduction

Hi. If you’re reading this, I just want you to know I am being chased by a peach thing and I don’t have much time to talk. If you want to hear HOW THE HECK THIS HAPPENED, Continue reading. Also, This is book one in my series so, I have one more word for you. HELP!!!!!!!!! (By the way, please continue)

Chapter 1: Earth

“Rolling!” “3… 2… 1… ACTION!” I was a director for a movie that me and my friends were making. It was called “The attack of the apes.” We were starting the 1st scene where the characters were introduced. We had been planning this for a long time and we were actually doing it. So, we started to film. It actually went pretty well! So, we started scene 2, where the ape breaks out of the zoo. And guess what happened, IT DIDN’T RECORD. So, we started it again. It finally worked this time. We took a brief intermission to grab chips and guacamole to celebrate completing 2 scenes already. We were talking about what should be for scene 3. Fred told me that it should be where the ape eats someone and everyone panics and runs for their lives! Todd suggested that it should be where the zookeeper's capture the ape with a net but the ape escapes from the net by breaking it and sets all the prisoners free at the jail by accident when he was really trying to eat the cell for food. So, we started to film. It was tedious, but turned out good. We followed the format. Plan, film. Plan, film Plan, Film. Plan, film until we reached scene 6. When the ape sees the camera and goes for it. But, I didn't see the curb until I fell. I braced for impact but I went through the ground and everything went black.

Chapter 2: Level 0

“Ughh…” “Where am I?” I woke up and I could feel something wet on my face, shirt, and pants. I stood up to find myself In a yellow room with buzzing fluorescent lights and a wet, moldy carpet. “Hello?” Nobody answered. I was starting to get worried about where my friends were. Just then, I saw something zip by that was black. I quickly hid in a dingy corner. I was going to panic. I turned around the corner to see a hideous, black creature staring right back at me! I was paralyzed in fear! It quickly ran around me, then ran away. I was confused. So, I ran with all my might until I found a note. It said, “Guide to escaping the Backroom’s entities. Duller: Stay still. But that’s all it said because the rest was ripped off. I checked the back. It said: “Go to different areas to find new techniques to escape different entities” Okay? I said and my journey was starting and I was pumped to get to the next level.

Chaper 3: The outer reaches of level 0

“ROAR!” “Ahhhhh!” There was a new creature. It was chasing me. I was running for my life. It was some sort of dog-creature and it sounded like a nightmare! I was screaming! I hid in a corner to catch my breath. It ran by and I was safe. I noticed something sticking out of the ground. It was, Almond water? I drank it because I was so thirsty. And suddenly, I felt refreshed! I continued walking until I found a slide. “What the…?” I thought. It said “Choose the right one. Get it right, continue. Get it wrong, DIE.” So, I chose the red one. A door locked behind me and I slid down. I slid down for what seemed like an hour. Then, I reached the exit of the slide. I looked at the blue slide and there was a grate that seemed to have you-know-whatted people. I ran into the depths of the underground area.

Chapter 4: The underground section

“Hello?” “Where am I?” I wandered around the darkened halls of the underground area until I heard something that sounded like a scream. I quickly turned around to see a hazmat suit on the ground. I put it on, just in case I needed it. So, I continued to wander around but then I saw a glowing light. I wondered what it was, so I stepped into the light.

Chapter 5: The kitchen

“Wait…” “Is that food?” I encountered a table and counters, pantries, an oven, a microwave, and more appliances for a kitchen. I opened all the drawers. No luck. So, I opened all the pantries. I got lucky. I saw a box of cheerios on the counter so I opened it and GUESS what was in it. AN ENTITY TRAP, ACTUALLY! So, I kept onto the entity trap and continued to look for food to take on the way. Then, I noticed something on the counter. I picked it up and it was automatically glued on the paper for the entities.It was for a… SMILER!?!?! “What is that!?” I thought to myself as I continued to look at the paper. It said, “The way to get rid of a smiler is by using, AN ENTITY TRAP like the one you found in the cheerio box.” “Oh!” I said. I found some candy bars and hit the road. Just then, I hit a wall and I suddenly was teleported to an area that was different from what I’ve seen before.

Chapter 6: The manila room

“Oh no…” “WHAT IS THAT!?!??!” I was running for my life from this… THING. Oh, And also, now that you all up to the story of what happened now you know why I’m running for my life. So, I ran for the walls in hope of teleporting out of here. And nothing happened so I ran for the door that the tan blob-of-a-human went into. I went in and I fell into a void of pitch black nothing. I could hear some ghostly whispers like “RUN…” “You don’t belong here” “YOU WILL *Censored*” I saw a ledge. I went for it and landed on it HARD. Then, I went black.

Chapter 7: The void

“...What happened?” “Where am I?” I stood up and remembered that I needed to get out of there. So, I opened the door and exited the void. ONLY TO BE BRUNG INTO A CREEPY ROOM! The room was eerie and quiet. I was worried about any entity around the premises. Suddenly a new entity emerged from the shadow. It had white eyes and it had a no body. It WAS A SMILER!!!!! I ran for my life and then suddenly I heard a voice “GET BACK!” The voice yelled. I turned around and it was an ASYNC employee! “Gotta go, see ya!” he said as he disappeared into the darkness.

The end
